Elsweyr Fondue

Description

Elsweyr Fondue got it's name from the location of its origin or from they use of Moon Sugar in the recipe. It's a cheesy meal best enjoyed with bread.

Ingredients

  • 1 Bottle of ale(flat)
  • Brown sugar
  • 100g Gruyere cheese(grated)
  • 100g Emmental cheese(grated)
  • 100g Soft Blue cheese(grated)

Steps

  1. Prep your dipping foods and set aside.
  2. On medium to low heat, bring the ale to a simmer.
  3. Lower the heat and begin adding the shredded cheeses a little at a time. Make sure all the cheese is incorporated before adding more.
  4. Slowly add in brown sugar to taste.
  5. Transfer your fondue, and enjoy!
