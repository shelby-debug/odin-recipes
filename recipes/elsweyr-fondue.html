Elsweyr Fondue

Description

Elsweyr Fondue got its name from the location of its origin or from they use of Moon Sugar in the recipe. It is a cheesy meal best enjoyed with bread.

Ingredients

  • 1 Bottle of ale(flat)
  • Brown sugar
  • 100g Gruyere cheese(grated)
  • 100g Emmental cheese(grated)
  • 100g Soft Blue cheese(grated)
